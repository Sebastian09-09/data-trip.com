msg:As a good hotel in Chongqing, it is necessary to give a good review.
The advantages first:
1. The environment of the hotel is very good. There is an exclusive canyon scenic spot at the back, which is quiet and original.
2. The landscape gardens in the hotel area seem to have taken great effort. Although the hotel is not big, the gardens are still relatively delicate.
3. The service is not bad. From check-in to meal to check-out, there are staff warmly serviced. It can be seen that the service concept is deeply rooted in the hotel.
There are also disadvantages:
1. The room area is not large, at most 40 square meters, which does not match this price.
2. The service details are not enough. The Chinese-style black tea ordered in the coffee shop is not brewed with a tea set, but with a mug. The turndown does not put water and water cups by the bed. These details are still not enough.
3 The advantages and disadvantages of catering are outstanding. The breakfast is very rich and has many varieties, but hard dishes such as steak and papaya snow clams are really not suitable for breakfast. The quality of the a la carte dishes for lunch and dinner is alright, but the prices are a bit unfriendly and far from the value of the dishes themselves.
4. Lack of gym.
Hope to do better.
