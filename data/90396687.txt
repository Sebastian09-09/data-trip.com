msg:The accommodations in Jiuru Mountain are as follows: Norman Fort and Mengzhen Shuxiang are together, as well as Muju, Cathouse and Wooden Barrel, as well as two tents. The tent experience is the worst. The one with the largest rooms and the most complete facilities here is Fort Norman. I stayed at Fort Norman for two nights this time and was very satisfied with my experience.

Fort Norman has an indoor swimming pool with very clear water. There were no other guests when I went there, so it was completely exclusive and the water quality was very good. In addition, there is a candle, perfume, lipstick, and soap making experience hall in the Norman Fort. Among the many experience halls, it feels like the most practical one. Living in a castle, it is very convenient to experience the above four places.

Starting from Fort Norman is the starting point of the last section of the climbing route. Living in Fort Norman, I started climbing at around nine o'clock after breakfast. The tourists hadn't come up yet, so I could enjoy the tranquility of the mountain to myself. When they reached the top and descended the mountain at around ten o'clock, the large group of tourists began to go up the mountain. It feels so good to climb a mountain at the wrong time. Come back from the mountain at 10:30, take a shower in the room, and go to various experience centers without delay.

The large balcony of Fort Norman is also very comfortable. In the evening, I can enjoy the mountain breeze on the balcony and watch the starry sky. In the morning, I can see the clouds and mist lingering on the top of the distant mountains. I feel that the hotel accommodation fee is worth it.

The service at the hotel front desk and all the waiters were very enthusiastic and their attitudes were very good. It was a very comfortable stay. Thumbs up!
