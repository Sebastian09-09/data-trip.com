msg:The software and hardware of the hotel are not mentioned. Although it is called a homestay, the software and hardware are basically based on the standards and models of five-star hotels. The layout of the rooms and the infinity pool are all designed by high-end hotels. The layout of the back mountain is different. Like the style of homestays, I feel that this is already a better model for homestays in the north. After all, star-rated hotels can meet the expectations of most people through a long-term experience summary, which is better than some so-called designers and whims. The homestay is more secure. In the past week, I saw some homestays, and most of them took pictures of the interior style. The exteriors were often dilapidated. Some Internet celebrity homestays were even built next to the national highway, surrounded by dilapidated houses.
There are several details in the hotel that are very good. First, in terms of water use, in addition to two bottles of VOSS, a bucket of Nongfu Spring is also given to make tea, which can fully meet the water consumption of a room for two days and one night. Second, although the room is not a parent-child room, children's slippers are still prepared. Third, there are three kinds of scented teas and one green tea in the room, which is also very distinctive. Fourth, the combs in the room are wooden combs, which can be reused. The sewing box is also a real sewing box, not the kind of cardboard with a few threads wrapped around it. The brand of toothpaste is also good, the only thing is that the toothbrush needs to be upgraded, which is uncomfortable to use. The bathroom supplies in the room should be Herborist. I think other rooms are. I don't know why my room is not, but because I don't care much, I didn't say anything. I guess they will change it for us immediately. Fifth, there will be a butler who will contact you after making a reservation, and will also set up a group for you to serve you. Each meal will be pre-ordered and the meal time. When the meal is ready, you will be notified to sit and serve, which is omitted. Waiting time, I forgot something after leaving the store, and I contacted and mailed it to us at the first time. The service can be said to be quite good. Sixth, the scenery is really good, the environment is good, and it can be seen that it is very attentive.
As for the suggestion, first, the food really needs to be improved. Although the price is expensive, it is not unacceptable. After all, the service, environment and positioning are kept here. It's hard to choose, the dishes are a bit biased, they are all hard dishes, there is a lack of adjus**ent, and they are too paranoid to take the high-end route. A mixed jelly should be called Chaohua Intangible Cultural Heritage Stupid Mung Bean Jelly with ten coriander juice. There are many dish names around the cross. In terms of price, if you don't order barbecue, it is about 100 per person. The barbecue is the most expensive I have ever eaten. A string of lamb skewers is 27 yuan, which is about 5 yuan for a string outside. . . . The second is that the acquiring side really needs to be improved. The dining mode is still in the mode of scanning the static code to pay for the photo. If you have a developer and need to connect with the bank for payment, you can come to me and do a little connection directly on the applet or the official account. If you pay for food, accommodation, and other items, you will pay more points, and you will be on the higher level. . .
There is still the possibility of re-living, after all, the experience is good.
